The beginning and meaning of Arbe


With nature.

Since when
1996,

Arbe is a compound word of "dawn" and "dream",
which means that you only dream in your own
quiet time at dawn. We started sharing scents to share
the dream Arbe aims for with everyone.

Vegan


Cosmetics refer to cosmetics that do not
undergo animal testing and use only
eco-friendly ingredients derived from nature
instead of animal-derived ingredients.

Vegan products do not contain any animal
ingredients and are not processed into animal
products, including honey, beeswax and
white sugar produced from animal bones.
